About

I am a data analyst and researcher with over a decade of experience in leveraging data to drive insights and inform strategic decision-making. My journey began with a Bachelor of Arts in Journalism from Temple University, where I developed a strong foundation in research and storytelling. During my time at Generocity, I authored over 400 news and feature stories, highlighting the work of nonprofits and social good enterprises. This experience honed my ability to communicate complex information effectively and manage multiple projects simultaneously.

In my current role as the Director of Research and Data at the Greater Philadelphia Cultural Alliance, I lead data collection and analysis initiatives that support the arts and culture sector. Notably, I spearheaded the 2024 Arts & Economic Prosperity report, partnering with Americans for the Arts and PA Humanities to assess the economic impact of the arts sector. Additionally, I directed the creation of research reports on the impact of COVID-19, providing critical insights to stakeholders. My technical skills, including proficiency in Python, SQL, Tableau, and other data visualization tools, have allowed me to automate processes, significantly improving efficiency and accuracy.

  • Tools & Statistics: Excel, Git/Github, Google Suite, Salesforce, Jupyter, MySQL
